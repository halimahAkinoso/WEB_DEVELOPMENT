About us

NeuroNova is a cutting-edge artificial intelligence company founded in 2021 by former OpenAI researchers. With a mission to build reliable, interpretable, and steerable Vision and Mission NeuroNova core belief is that AI should be aligned with human values and safety.

Unlike many companies racing to build the most powerful models, NeuroNova focuses on constitutional AI—a technique that trains models to follow a set of guiding principles, making them more predictable and ethical.
