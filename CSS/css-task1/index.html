NeuroNova Company Overview NeuroNova AB is a pioneering biotechnology company based in Stockholm, Sweden, dedicated to developing transformative therapies for neurodegenerative diseases. Founded in 1998, NeuroNova has built a reputation for innovation in drug discovery, focusing on neurogenesis—the process of generating new neurons in the adult brain—as a therapeutic strategy. Mission To revolutionize the treatment of central nervous system disorders by harnessing the power of neural stem cells and novel molecular targets, addressing conditions with high unmet medical need. Core Focus Areas - Parkinson’s Disease: Development of sNN0031, a promising candidate for moderate to severe stages. - Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS): Advancement of sNN0029, aimed at slowing disease progression. - Neurogenesis-Based Therapies: Leveraging adult neural stem cells to regenerate damaged brain tissue.Scientific Innovation NeuroNova’s research integrates cutting-edge neuroscience with proprietary technologies to develop small molecules and proteins that stimulate brain repair. The company holds over 80 patents, with several active filings targeting the melanocortin 4 receptor—a key player in neural regulation. Strategic Milestones - Acquired by Newron Pharmaceuticals in 2012, enabling broader clinical development and commercialization. - Secured over $19 million in funding, including multiple rounds of venture capital and grants. - Collaborated with leading institutions and investors such as HealthCap and Novare Kapital.
